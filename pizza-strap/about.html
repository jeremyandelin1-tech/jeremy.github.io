Sumthin' About Pizza

Here at Uncle Ben’s Ratscallion's Pizza place we like to have somewhat of a certain "je ne sais quoi" that makes you feel as if you should've taken the stairs instead of hopping on that elevator if you know what I’m talkin’ about.

*I Miss You* by Blink-182 is our only vinyl we have playing in the bathroom and unfortunately it’s stuck on repeat over that weird section where they start talking about spiders.

We had a "Chuck E Cheese" style mascot that used to do some sort of act out front but he's out on house arrest for the next few months, so the pinball machines are kinda our main attraction for the time being.

Hope you got your tetanus and flu shots, because, oh well, they’re just important to have in general. Hope the pie’s not too cold. Cheers mate!
